Welcome to Pappas K. & M. O.E.

Pappas K. & M. O.E Company was founded in 1997 at Piraeus and has operated as a merchandiser of mechanical sealing products ever since.

The basis of our success lies in three principles: product quality, customer support and specialised personnel.

Quality of products is one of our main concerns. This is why our company imports and distributes only those products that comply with the strictest ISO 9001 standards. All our products bear the ISO 9001 Cerification.sides product quality, we also understand the importance of customer services, which means outstanding customer support from our licensed engineers as well as the immediate supply of products in stock at our head office in Piraeus. Our company imports a large variety of products to ensure maximum sufficiency of the products in demand.

Through our yearlong experience in the field, we have acquired the necessary expertise that enables us to offer valuable advice based on your company needs. Pappas K. & M. O.E aim at exceeding customer expectations, through our commitment to continuous customer support and attention to the quality of our products and services.
